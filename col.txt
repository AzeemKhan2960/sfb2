Collections:
-List(indexed,duplicates,insertion order)
 -ArrayList(continues memory allocation)
 -LinkedList(random memory allocation having associated address to next value)
-Set(non-indexed,unique)
 -HashSet(random order)
 -LinkedHashSet(insertion order)
 -TreeSet(ascending)
-Map(2D,custom-indexed,key-value)
 -HashMap(random order of key)
 -LinkedHashMap(insertion order of keys)
 -TreeMap(ascending order of keys)
